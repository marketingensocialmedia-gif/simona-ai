FAQ

1) Does this replace my front desk?

No. Simona AI supports your team by capturing and organizing leads when staff is busy or after-hours—so your front desk can focus on patients/clients in front of them.

2) What happens if someone wants a human right away?

We add “human handoff” triggers. If the caller asks for a person, is upset, or it’s urgent, Simona routes them to your preferred option.

3) Can it handle urgent dental calls?

Yes. Urgent keywords (pain, swelling, bleeding, emergency) can trigger priority routing instructions and immediate staff alerts.

4) Can it answer after-hours?

Yes. You choose the after-hours message and next steps, including capturing details and sending an SMS so the lead doesn’t go cold.

5) Will patients/clients get annoyed by texts?

Texts are short and helpful. We include an opt-out (“Reply STOP to opt out”) and only message people who contact your practice.

6) How fast can we launch?

Typically 48–72 hours once we have your basic info (hours, services, routing rules, and staff notification preferences).

7) Do you integrate with my systems?

We can log leads to a Google Sheet and connect to common tools depending on your setup. On the demo we’ll confirm what you use and the best integration path.

8) What if Simona doesn’t understand a question?

We design a safe fallback: clarify once, then route to your team or capture the lead and promise a callback—so you still save the opportunity.

9) Is this compliant? Do you record calls?

We can configure call recording and disclosures based on your preference and local requirements. We also include clear privacy and SMS consent language on the website.

10) What does “qualified lead” mean?

A qualified lead is a real person who provides name + phone and a clear reason for calling, with a preferred callback time (and optional details like insurance/treatment interest).
